Our Sports Philosophy

At Nalanda High School, we believe that sports play a vital role in a student's holistic development.

Our sports program is designed to build character, promote teamwork, and instill discipline while developing physical fitness. We emphasize participation, sportsmanship, and personal growth over winning at all costs.

Through sports, our students learn valuable life lessons like resilience, leadership, time management, and the importance of hard work and dedication. Our coaches mentor students to become not just better athletes, but better individuals.

Our Sports Programs

A wide range of sporting opportunities for all students

Cricket

Our cricket team has a strong tradition of excellence, with regular participation in district and state-level tournaments.

Basketball

Our basketball program focuses on developing skills, teamwork, and strategic thinking both on and off the court.

Athletics

Our athletics program covers track and field events with opportunities for individual excellence and personal records.

Volleyball

Our volleyball program emphasizes teamwork, coordination, and quick decision-making skills.

Badminton

Our badminton program helps develop hand-eye coordination, agility, and mental toughness.

Chess

Our chess program focuses on strategic thinking, planning, and concentration skills.

We are dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing the potential of every student in a safe and supportive environment.
