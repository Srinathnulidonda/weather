Our Core Values

Respect

We build a caring community in which every child is valued, respected and encouraged to share the gifts and talents he or she possesses.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in education, enabling students to achieve their best through high quality teaching and learning.

Integrity

We embrace honesty, accountability, and ethical behavior that guides students to make responsible choices in all aspects of life.

Collaboration

We believe that partnerships between students, teachers, parents, and the community create an inclusive learning environment.

Innovation

We promote and develop creative thinking and critical thinking skills, equipping students to navigate and succeed within a dynamic world.

Compassion

We promote kindness, empathy and understanding, creating a school community based on care and respect for others.

Diversity

Celebrate difference and promote inclusion, fostering an environment where everyone feels welcome and recognized for their unique background and perspectives.

Education as a Lifetime Journey

We are building passion for learning which extends beyond the class to become a way of life.

Our Vision

At Nalanda High School, we are committed to fostering a foundation of confidence, creativity, and capability in our students, empowering them to excel and reach new heights in their chosen fields within our ever-evolving world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help every child become a lifelong learner in a safe and caring environment. Through belief, initiative, courage, and passion, we encourage students to grow through learning, skills, sports, and service, striving for excellence in all they do.

We are dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing the potential of every student in a safe and supportive environment.
