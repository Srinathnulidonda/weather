High School Student Life

At Nalanda High School, we believe in developing well-rounded individuals

Competitive Sports

Our high school offers competitive sports teams that compete at district and state levels, including basketball, volleyball, cricket, athletics, and more. Students develop discipline, teamwork, and leadership through competitive sports.

Community Service

Students participate in meaningful service projects including teaching at local schools, environmental cleanup initiatives, and support for underprivileged communities. These experiences foster empathy, social responsibility, and leadership skills.

Leadership Programs

Our student government, debate team, and Model UN program develop critical leadership skills. Students organize school events, participate in interschool competitions, and gain confidence in public speaking and decision-making.
