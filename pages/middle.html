Developing Independent Thinkers

At Nalanda Middle School, we guide students through a crucial period of academic and personal growth.

Our middle school program is specifically designed to meet the unique needs of adolescents, providing challenging academics while supporting social and emotional development. We create an environment where students can explore their interests and develop their identities.

Middle School Student Life

At Nalanda, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom

Athletics & Sports

Our comprehensive athletics program develops teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness through a variety of competitive and recreational sports including basketball, volleyball, cricket, and track & field.

Clubs & Activities

Students can explore their interests through various clubs and activities including science club, debate team, art society, robotics, coding club, and student council, fostering leadership and community involvement.

Field Trips & Excursions

Our educational excursions enrich classroom learning through real-world experiences at museums, historical sites, scientific institutions, and cultural events, connecting academic concepts to the world around us.

Arts & Cultural Events

Students express themselves through our vibrant arts program including annual plays, music concerts, dance performances, and art exhibitions, celebrating creativity and cultural diversity in our community.
