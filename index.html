About Our School

Founded in 2004, Nalanda High School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Armoor.

We are dedicated to providing a holistic educational experience that balances academic rigor with character development. Our experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and innovative teaching methodologies create an environment where students thrive intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

Our Academic Programs

Discover the diverse educational pathways we offer to nurture young minds

Elementary School

Building strong foundations for lifelong learning.

Our elementary program focuses on nurturing curiosity and developing fundamental skills through engaging, activity-based learning. Students explore core subjects while developing creativity and critical thinking skills.

  • Phonics-based reading program
  • Hands-on mathematics
  • Interactive science exploration
  • Arts and music integration
Learn More

Middle School

Guiding students through crucial developmental years.

Our middle school program balances academic rigor with social-emotional development. Students explore more advanced concepts in core subjects while developing independence and critical thinking skills.

  • Advanced mathematics and science
  • Literature and language arts
  • Social studies and history
  • Technology and digital literacy
Learn More

High School

Preparing students for college and future success.

Our high school program offers rigorous academics with specialized pathways. Students receive personalized guidance as they prepare for higher education and develop skills essential for their future careers.

  • Advanced science and mathematics
  • Literature and composition
  • Career and college counseling
  • Leadership and civic engagement
